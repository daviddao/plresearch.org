Neurotech is dedicated to accelerating neurotechnologies for the benefit of humanity. A critical focus of this area is the development and responsible advancement of brain-computer interfaces (BCI), which represent one of the most profound frontiers in computing today.

As part of our mission to drive breakthroughs that push humanity forward, we aim to steer collective energy toward ensuring positive outcomes in these transformative areas. By navigating potential pitfalls and harnessing the full potential of neurotech, we can shape a future where technology deeply integrated with human cognition empowers society.
