Molly leads the Protocol Dev, Product Dev, and Research Dev teams at Protocol Labs. Her work includes protocol design and development for the **IPFS Project**, the **Filecoin Network**, **libp2p**, **IPLD**, and **Drand**.

She is also the CEO of **FilOz**, focusing on the long-term evolution and security of the Filecoin protocol. Previously, she was a Product Manager at Google working on Search, Books, and Education. She holds a degree in Computer Science from Stanford University.
